OciXCrom's Rank System: List of keywords for the .ini file
Part 1: HUD information (used in HUDINFO_FORMAT and HUDINFO_FORMAT_FINAL)
  • $current_xp$: the amount of XP the player currently has
  • $next_xp$: the amount of XP that the next level requires
  • $xp_needed$: the amount of XP that the player needs for the next level
  • $level$: player's current level
  • $next_level$: the number of the next level
  • $rank$: player's current rank
  • $next_rank$: the name of the next rank
  • $max_levels$: the number of available levels
  • $br$: moves the text on a new line
  • $name$: player's name
    Part 2: Special events (used in [XP Rewards])
  • kill: the amount of XP received for a normal kill
  • headshot: the amount of XP received for a headshot kill
  • teamkill: the amount of XP received for killing a teammate
  • suicide: the amount of XP received for commiting suicide
  • death: the amount of XP received for dying
  • bomb_planted: the amount of XP received for planting the bomb
  • bomb_defused: the amount of XP received for defusing the bomb
  • bomb_exploded: the amount of XP received for a successful bomb explosion
  • team_win: the amount of XP received for winning a round
  • team_lose: the amount of XP received for losing a round
